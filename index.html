About Us

Born in the Mission District of San Francisco, Mission Hot Club is an eclectic acoustic band playing Gypsy Jazz and Dixieland with forays into Musette, Klezmer and related genres. We are an international ensemble with members hailing from France, Eastern Europe and beyond. We bring our music to bars, restaurants, parks, art galleries, book stores and homes around the Bay Area.
